Improving your numeracy

So far in this course we’ve seen how numbers and data are everywhere. And we’ve looked at how if you can figure out how to pull valuable and meaningful info from all these numbers and data, you’ll be able to make way better and much more informed decisions. Some of these decisions will be important, as they could support the strategy and direction of your business.

Now we’re going to look at metrics, ratio’s and percentages and how they can help your numeracy skills. This is about how you use numbers to gather insights. After all, numbers alone mean nothing if we can’t pull out the info that helps us make better decisions!

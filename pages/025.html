Challenge thinking

Traditionally, being excellent at maths has meant that you have mastered all the complicated stuff like trigonometry, calculus and algebra. In reality that’s the sort of thing hardly anyone remembers later on in life –mainly because we don’t tend to use it very much.But lots of people don’t agree with this view of maths. It’s a view that probably led to lots of us being less skilled at core maths operations ( +, -, X, /, fractions, decimals, percentages etc) – which is a really valuable lifetime skill.

And that’s why we developed this course. Businesses today are looking for everyday practical numeracy skills, but they seem to be struggling to find them. Luckily, you’re now in the right place to get to grips with the skills that are going to be most valuable to you (and your future employers) as you enter the workforce!

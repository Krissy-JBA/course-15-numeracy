Zoey's Numeracy needs.

Being numerate means being able to deal with numbers and other mathematical concepts - and to be able to apply these in a range of situations to solve a variety of problems. Being numerate is as much about thinking and reasoning logically, as it is about 'doing sums'.

What is numeracy?

So, you’ve seen all the competencies and skills that Zoey needs in her career. Now can you see how having good numeracy skills would be great for her? Numeracy’s one of those everyday skills that’s hard for any of us to do without - either at work or in our personal lives.

Remember, by numeracy we simply mean the ability to use maths in everyday life.

By being numerate you’ll have the confidence and skill to use numbers and mathematical approaches in every area of your life. This can be at work, in practical everyday activities at home, as everyday consumers, in managing finances and as parents helping their children.

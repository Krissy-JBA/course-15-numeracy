What is a ratio?

A ratio is a metric that shows a relationship between two numbers.

It’s basically a comparison of two things. We might compare boys to girls, cars to trucks or hours asleep to hours awake.

Whatever’s being compared can be written out as a ratio – and we can write different types of ratios.

A ratio can be written as a to b, a:b or a/b

The order you write the ratio is also important.

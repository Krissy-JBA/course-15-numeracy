What is a ratio?

Example: Zoey has a sewing container with 4 buttons and 5 needles.

The order you write the ratio is important.

Write a ratio of buttons to needles. 4:7

Write a ratio of needles to buttons. 7:4

Notice that the order of the numbers depends on the order of what you want to compare.

Write a ratio of buttons to total shapes. 4:11

Write a ratio of total shapes to buttons. 11:4

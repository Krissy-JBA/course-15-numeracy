Sales targets

Are you meeting the sales targets you set? How about the quantity targets – are you meeting them as well as meeting your $ targets? A business can meet its $ targets by slashing prices and selling its product cheaply. But meeting targets this way sacrifices profitability, and so isn’t very good as a long term strategy!

If you’re meeting your targets, identify what’s going well and try to do more of whatever it is!

If you’re not meeting targets, are you missing out on customer numbers, volume of product and $? Focus on both the strongest and the weakest area. Try to make the strongest area better, and to improve (or eliminate) the weakest area.

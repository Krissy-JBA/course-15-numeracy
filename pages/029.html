Model fitting day

With paid models coming in and limited hours available, Zoey needs to make some decisions. How many models are coming? How much time does she have? How long does it take for a model to get changed? How long will alterations take? How many times will a model need to change their outfit? What if someone’s sick? How many staff will Zoey need? By doing some maths, Zoey can come up with a plan to really get the best out of the day.

Workplace numeracy

Now we’re going to help Zoey with numeracy in relation to her fashion line. So what is numeracy in the workplace?

As you start to build your career, your ability to do basic maths (and to interpret that data to make decisions that are good for business) is going to be essential.

Think about this quick scenario: Zoey’s got a number of supermodels coming in on a particular day to try on some outfits for her show. These are paid models, so think about what Zoey needs to do to get the best value out of that day.

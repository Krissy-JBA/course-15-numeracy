What is a percentage

The word ‘per cent’ comes from the Latin ‘per centum’, which just means ‘out of a hundred’ in English. It’s shown by the symbol %.

Example: Below is a grid made up of 100 squares. The coloured sections are made up of various amounts of squares out of 100. This means the shapes cover different percentages of the grid.

  • 1% is orange

    (1 out 100)

  • 5% is yellow

    (5 out 100)

  • 10% is purple

    (10 out of 100)

  • 25% is green

    (25 out of 100)

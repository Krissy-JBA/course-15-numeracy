Apps store

We live in a world where many of us own a smartphone. Which is handy as it means we’ve got 100’s of awesome apps at our fingertips. And many of those apps can be used to help improve your numeracy skills - everything from Sudoku puzzles to problem solving apps.

One example is Maths WorkOut - a set of daily brain training exercises and math drills designed to improve your mental arithmetic skills.

Look for apps that send you daily notifications. You’re more likely to do the exercises if they come to you!

Number symbols

Some numbers stand for amounts, like prices. Others, like phone numbers, don’t!

Decimal numbers

The digit to the right of the decimal point represents tenth parts - so 1.03 actually means one and three tenths.

Negative numbers

Negative numbers (i.e. the ones with a minus sign in front of them – not ones that are being miserable!) are used to count below zero. -1 is one below 0

Rounding numbers

Rounding a number makes it easier to work with, it also makes it easier to estimate, or ‘best guess’ unknown amounts.

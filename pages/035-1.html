Floor Maths

The total floor area of the studio is 6metres by 6metres which equals 36 square metres.

We can ignore the bits that won’t be covered, i.e. the storage areas and toilet.

So as an equation, it looks like this:

- (Toilet area + Storage area)

- ( 4m2 + 6m2 )

- 10 m2

The answer for the builder is that the area to be covered is 24 square metres

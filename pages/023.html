Numeracy VS Math

Numeracy is all about being able to to reason and to apply simple numerical concepts. If you’ve got basic numeracy skills then you can understand fundamental arithmetic like addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.

Maths is seen when;

Calculating the numbers

Using formulas to work out the weekly or monthly spend

Dividing the numbers throughout the budget

Numeracy is seen when;

You’re able to understand the budget

When using it in real life situations

When working out whether you’ve gone over your budget

Zoey's Budget

When Zoey creates a budget for a project, you could say that the maths component is when she’s calculating the numbers, using formulas to work out the weekly or monthly spend and dividing the numbers throughout the budget.

As for numeracy, that’s when Zoey’s able to understand and apply that budget, when she’s using it in real life situations and when she’s working out whether she’s gone over her budget.

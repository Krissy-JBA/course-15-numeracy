Floor Maths

Yellow

4 ÷ 20 x 100 = 0.20 x .100 = 20%

Orange

4 ÷ 20 x 100 = 0.20 x .100 = 20%

Red

5 ÷ 20 x 100 = 0.25 x .100 = 25%

So we know that our fabric rolls are :

Blue 35%

Yellow 20%

Orange 20%

Red 25%

This adds up tp 100%

Hint: For each colour divide the quantity of that colour over total quantity, then multiply by 100.

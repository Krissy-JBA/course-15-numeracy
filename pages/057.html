Part 2 - Zoey’s data

“What is data?” That’s what Zoey said when she was asked what she does with all her data! When people talk about data, they’re usually referring to data as a group of individual pieces of info that hasn’t been processed yet …you could say the data is raw. As an example, Zoey’s studio address is a piece of data and when lots of pieces of data are gathered together, they can be analysed and grouped together to become info, creating something like Google Maps. The info can then be used to gain knowledge such as “How do I get to the address of Yezo Clothing”?

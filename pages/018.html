Zoey's Fashion Career

Zoey is a fashion designer! She completed her fashion degree a year ago and started a fashion business operating out of a small studio. She’s got one assistant and two part time fashion students that come in and help. She’s got a steady income from a line of clothing under her fashion label ‘Yezo Clothing’. You might be able to figure out where that name came from. Zoey’s been working hard to bring her vision to life, has a really unique style and has designed a range of trendy clothes. But she’s the first to admit she’s not the best with numbers. And that’s a concern because the fashion design business isn’t catwalks and clothes! It’s full of skills and competencies that need you to have a really good understanding of numbers.

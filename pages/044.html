Intro to measurements

Measuring isn’t just about using a ruler. Businesses measure many different things in different ways for many different reasons – and they’re all important for Zoey if she’s going to be successful.

There’s a well known business mantra that says “if you can measure it, you can manage it”. A key factor for success in business is knowing what to measure. Many businesses make the mistake of measuring the wrong things, or just measuring things that are easy to measure.

Data

As a business, Yezo Clothing is made up of lots of data. Customer details, sales invoices and purchase invoices are just some of Zoey’s basic records that contain loads of business data.

Information

Information is data that’s been given meaning. In Zoey’s business, information might be spreadsheets of all the types and quantities of fabrics, buttons, cotton and zips she has in the studio.

Knowledge

Knowledge is an awareness of that information. For example Zoey knows what stock levels of fabrics, buttons, cotton and zips are in the studio. With knowledge comes understanding!

Wisdom

When Zoey has understanding, she then has wisdom and can make decisions and judgements that are best for her fashion business. For example she can take the knowledge of stock levels and decide when she needs to reorder fabrics. Wisdom is seeking out the right information, and choosing the right business direction and strategy.

From data to wisdom

Data, information, knowledge and wisdom form a hierarchy of different but related concepts. It’s all about turning data into information that gives you knowledge about your business that you you didn’t have before. You can then take this knowledge and make decisions based on your new-found wisdom.
